This guide is designed to help authors and developers get up and running quickly with Methanol, focusing on content creation with minimal configuration overhead.

## Standard Workflow

1) Execute the [Quick Start](../quick-start) to initialize your environment.
2) Create your homepage at `pages/index.mdx`.
3) Progressively add content files and configure their Frontmatter.
4) Build the site and publish the contents of the `dist/` directory.
